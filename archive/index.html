Era.js

Now:

Meet EraJs. A library that helps you to estimate how long an era of time is occupied. It takes weekends, half days, vacation and hollidays into account when calculating.

Creating a new Era.

var myEra = new Era();

Adding estimated hours to your Era.

myEra.addHours(320);

Adding vacation days to your Era.

myEra.addVacationDays(2);

Requesting the end of your era.

myEra.end();

Calling the previous function will return the end of your Era.
